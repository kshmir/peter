Peter is a launcher designed for seniors and people with cognitive impairment. It replaces the home screen with a simple large-icon interface, showing only caregiver-approved apps.

KEY FEATURES:

• Simplified home screen with large icons and readable text
• Only shows apps approved by the caregiver
• Automatic blocking of unauthorized app installs
• Protection against suspicious WhatsApp links
• Spam/scam notification filtering
• Emergency contacts with direct calling
• PIN-protected admin panel
• System text size adjustment
• Spanish and English support

DESIGNED FOR:

• Seniors with technology difficulties
• People with mild cognitive impairment or dementia
• Families wanting to protect their loved ones

SECURITY:

• Unapproved apps are automatically uninstalled
• Suspicious WhatsApp links are intercepted
• Spam messages are filtered before delivery
• Admin access requires a 4-digit PIN
• Full security event log

Peter was built with love to protect those we care about most.
