Your grandmother deserves a phone that keeps her safe. Peter is a secure launcher built by a caregiver, for caregivers -- designed to protect seniors with dementia and cognitive impairment from scams, fraud, and confusing apps.

Peter replaces the home screen with a calm, simple interface showing only the apps you approve. Behind the scenes, ElderGuard -- our intelligent security engine -- watches over every notification, message, and phone call so your loved one never has to worry.

ELDERGUARD SECURITY:

Scam & Fraud Detection
Peter intercepts suspicious WhatsApp messages, SMS, and notifications before they reach your loved one. Our detection engine recognizes financial fraud patterns across Latin America, Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and Africa -- from fake prize scams to bank phishing to crypto fraud.

Call Screening
Calls from unknown or suspicious numbers are silenced automatically. Your loved one only receives calls from contacts you approve.

Auto-Reply to Scammers
When a scam message is detected, Peter can automatically respond to the sender, wasting the scammer's time and protecting your family member without any action needed.

WhatsApp Protection
Suspicious links, unknown contacts, and phishing attempts inside WhatsApp are caught and quarantined before delivery.
